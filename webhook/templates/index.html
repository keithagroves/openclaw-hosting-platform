Frequently asked questions

What is OpenClaw?

OpenClaw is an AI-powered automation tool that runs on your desktop. With OpenClaw Hosting, you get a cloud desktop with OpenClaw already installed so you can start using it immediately without any local setup.

How do I access my desktop?

After signing up, you'll receive a unique desktop URL. Visit it in any browser and sign in with the Google account you used to subscribe. No passwords to remember, no software to install.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Each desktop runs in its own isolated container with its own private network. All connections are encrypted with HTTPS. Your files are stored on persistent volumes with daily backups.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel your subscription anytime from your Stripe billing portal. There are no contracts or cancellation fees.

What happens to my files if I cancel?

When your subscription ends, your desktop is shut down. Your data is retained for 30 days in case you want to resubscribe. After that, it's permanently deleted.
